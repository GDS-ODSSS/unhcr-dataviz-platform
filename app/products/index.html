Products

Browse through the collection of storymaps, dashboards and infographics created by UNHCR’s Information Product Development and Analysis unit. These so-called “IM products” are often produced in collaboration with other UNHCR teams, both in headquarters and around the world. They focus on different topics related to UNHCR’s work and mandate as well as different countries and regions facing displacement crises.
Perhaps they can inspire your data storytelling?
